About Theobroma Patisserie

Theobroma stores have been spreading sweetness and smiles across the nation for over a decade and a half, and now our online bakery welcomes you to partake in our delectable offerings from the comfort of your home. At Theobroma, you’re treated to a wide range of baked delicacies, from exquisite cakes to artisanal breads and everything in between. If you’re looking to order bakery products online, look no further than Theobroma, for our curated collection of baked goods has something for everyone and every mood!

About Us

The word Theobroma literally translates to ‘Food of the Gods’, where ‘Theos’ means Gods and ‘broma’ means food. Coincidentally, it is also the scientific name of the cocoa plant (Theobroma cacao), which is the star ingredient in many of the products offered by this patisserie. Apart from our stellar collection of sweet items, like pastries, cakes, cupcakes, chocolates, croissants and cookies, we also offer delectable savouries such as puffs, quiches, cakes, wraps, and rolls. With our well-organised website and the easy ‘order online’ feature, we make ordering bakery products online a piece of cake!

Our History

We started off as a tiny bakery store at Cusrow Baug on Colaba Causeway, Mumbai and have come a long way from our humble beginnings. We are now a professionally-run, pan-India bakery and patisserie that is constantly striving to innovate and provide the best in the world of baked goods. Since 2004, Theobroma has been diligently expanding its presence in metros, and we now have stores in Mumbai, Pune, Delhi NCR, Bengaluru and Hyderabad. Our goal was and always has been to provide the best bakery products to people across the country and we continue to chase that goal. Theobroma currently has over 80 outlets across India and our journey has just begun!

Whether you step into a Theobroma store or shop from our online bakery shop, you will see our guiding principles shine through our wide range of high-quality products and personalised service.

  • Authentic Recipes – The baked delicacies offered by Theobroma are based on and inspired by decades-old home-style recipes that are made only from the freshest of ingredients and without the use of artificial preservatives and chemicals.
  • Passionately Baked – At Theobroma, we don’t sell what we don’t eat ourselves. And this has been the guiding motto of our bakers and chefs right from our inception. All our products are crafted with love, care, and the best standards of hygiene.
  • Honestly Priced –Theobroma products strike the perfect balance between cost-effectiveness and superior quality so that everyone can enjoy what we have to offer.
  • Quality First – We never, never compromise on quality, be it in terms of ingredients or in our customer service.

Theobroma Bestsellers

Theobroma has a vast and wonderful assortment of desserts, snacks, and savouries. But there are a few products that enjoy a little more love that the others on the menu. Let’s look at some of our customer favourites.

Chocoholic cake: This tri-layered cake combines dark, white and milk chocolate mousse to give you an explosion of flavours. If you love chocolate, do not miss trying this!

Chocolate tart: A crispy, buttery pastry shell encases a rich chocolate ganache made of pure chocolate. Every bite into this tart gives you a mouthful of chocolate. No wonder our customers find it irresistible.

Millionaire brownie: If you have a sweet-tooth, this is a sandwich you have to taste. In this layered treat, a soft buttery caramel lies nestled between a chocolate chip brownie and a layer of rich dark chocolate truffle.
